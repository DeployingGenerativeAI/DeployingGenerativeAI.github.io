Learning from Medical Time Series

Workshop at NeurIPS 2022


Invited Speakers Organizers

Time series data are ubiquitous in healthcare (from medical time series to wearable data) and present an exciting opportunity for machine learning methods to extract actionable insights about human health. However, gaps remain to make machine learning systems practical and deployable for healthcare. This is because learning from medical time series is notoriously challenging: labels are often noisy or missing, data are extremely high dimensional, missing values are pervasive, measurements are irregular, data distributions shift rapidly over time, explaining the model outcome is challenging, and deployed models require careful maintenance over time. Most of these challenges are still an open question and an active area of research with significant room for contribution. When considered together, learning from time series for health is a uniquely challenging and important area; Significant advancements are required to realize the societal benefits of ML systems for healthcares. We hope to investigate myriad questions during the workshop.
